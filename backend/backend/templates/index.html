About GDP

Gross Domestic Product (GDP) is a measure of the economic health and growth of a country. It represents the total monetary value of all goods and services produced within a nation during a specific period.

GDP is a crucial indicator for policymaking, helping governments design effective economic strategies and budgets.

Higher GDP often signifies greater economic activity, innovation, and development across various industries.

Understanding GDP trends enables businesses and individuals to make informed decisions about investments and resources.
